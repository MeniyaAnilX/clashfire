Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026. Welcome to FreeDiamond.in. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data we collect. This Privacy Policy details how we handle user information safely.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal identities like your legal name, physical address, email, or passwords. The only inputs we capture are: (a) Your numerical Garena Free Fire Player UID to execute diamond rewards top-ups, and (b) An automated anonymous browser device identifier generated locally to prevent invalid automated traffic or bot registrations.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

We use standard browser local storage configurations and secure functional cookies to track active task progress, cache reward claims, and verify session tokens. Third-party ad providers (such as Monetag or Google Adsense) may also deploy cookies to display tailored interest-based advertisements.

3. Data Security

All user coins balances and tasks history are encrypted and synchronized securely via Firebase Firestore database protocols. We never sell, rent, or distribute player UIDs or device tokens to external third-party data brokers.
